Game objective

The objective of the game is to get a high score by eliminating as many balls as possible in a single stroke. If you eliminate only two balls, you will score zero points. If you eliminate three balls, you will score one point. The more balls you eliminate in a single mouse click the higher your score will be.

To faciliate the task of spotting the ball clusters, Same GNOME will animate the cluster of balls when the mouse is over them (this also indicates that if you click on the cluster, it will eliminate the balls)

Eliminating all of the balls on the board will grant you an extra one thousand points
